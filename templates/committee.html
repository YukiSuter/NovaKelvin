Leading the Kelvin Symphony Orchestra

What We Do

The Kelvin Symphony Orchestra is entirely student-run, and our committee is the driving force behind everything we do. From organizing rehearsals and concerts to managing finances and promoting our events, the committee ensures that KSO continues to thrive as one of Glasgow's premier student orchestras.

Our committee members are passionate musicians and dedicated volunteers who work tirelessly to create opportunities for students to perform, learn, and enjoy orchestral music together. Each role is vital to our success, and we're proud of the collaborative spirit that makes KSO special.
