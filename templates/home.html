University of Glasgow's Student Orchestra

The Kelvin Symphony Orchestra, established 1990 as "The Kelvin Ensemble" is the University of Glasgow's student orchestra, with musicians from across many disciplines.

Our talented musicians, comprising students and faculty, of universities in the Glasgow area, come together to create unforgettable musical experiences that celebrate orchestral and chamber music.

Whether you're a longtime classical music enthusiast or discovering the orchestra for the first time, we invite you to join us for an evening of extraordinary music.
